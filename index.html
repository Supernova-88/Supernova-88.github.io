SUPERNOVA-88-ltkef

Supernova 88, the pulsating music club, ignited its cosmic journey on November 14, 2019, within the vibrant walls of Lycee Ahmed Amara El Kef. Guided by the celestial tunes of "Remember" (نتفكر), the anthems of unity in "We Are Here" (نحن هنا), and the fiery passion of "Fire in Me" (فيا نار), the club became a celestial sanctuary where beats resonate and memories are etched in the cosmic tapestry of sound. In the heart of Supernova 88, the club's ethos echoes the harmonies of "بين يديك" and "In Your Hands," creating a timeless musical experience.
